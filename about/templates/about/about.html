Ignite Theatre

Newhaven's new theatre company Ignite Theatre are committed to delivering high calibre, affordable theatre to Newhaven and surrounding areas. We work with local community groups and create opportunities to be involved.

For us, nothing can beat the buzz of feeling inspired and moved... to share laughter, outrage, sadness or glee as a shared experience.

To enjoy a slice of life in a space, where a part of the story is your own.

A space, an audience, some actors and a story are the elements to make some magic happen in this vintage venue at Meeching Hall for our kinsfolk.
