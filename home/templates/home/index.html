 A Grandma's GRIMM

Ignite Theatre present 'A Grandma's Grimm' a musical, story-theatre extravaganza. Expect golden apples, frog princesses and spinning wheels - all in praise of the Grandmothers who gave their stories to the Brother's Grimm. Written and directed by Xanthe Gresham Knight with music from Kate Daisy Grant

Also including 'The Breman Town Musicians' from Haven Young Creative, directed by Siou Hanamm and the Norcross Dancers choreographed by Alison Norcross.
